Intel Xeon E5-2650 v3 @ 2.30GHz:

P_{core} = 4 lanes x 2 flop x 2 FMA x 2.30 GHz = 36.8 GFlops/s
P_{CPU}  = 10 cores x P_{core}                 = 368 GFlops/s
P_{node} = 2 sockets x P_{CPU}                 = 736 GFlops/s
P_{EVII} = 42 nodes x P_{node}                 = 30,912 GFlops/s
                                               = 30.912 TFlops/s
